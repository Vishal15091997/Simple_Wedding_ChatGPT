How Destiny Found Love

Two families met, and a simple introduction began what would become a beautiful journey. What started as an arranged meeting slowly unfolded into something far more meaningful. Conversations turned into laughter, moments turned into memories, and a quiet understanding grew between two hearts. In time, what destiny arranged, love embraced. Today we celebrate not just a wedding, but a journey where friendship, respect, and affection blossomed into something truly special.

Together with Simoni, he begins a new chapter built on companionship, trust, and shared dreams.
